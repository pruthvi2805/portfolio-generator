Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device. Always.

Portfolio Builder runs 100% in your browser. We never collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information.

No Data Collection

All portfolio data you enter remains exclusively in your browser's local storage. We have no servers that receive or store your information. When you download your portfolio, the files are generated entirely on your device.

No Tracking

We don't use analytics, cookies, or any tracking mechanisms. Your browsing activity on this site is completely private.

No Account Required

You don't need to create an account or provide any personal information to use Portfolio Builder. Just open the site and start building.

Local Storage

Your draft portfolio data is saved to your browser's local storage so you can continue where you left off. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

Open Source

Portfolio Builder is open source. You can verify our privacy practices by reviewing the code on GitHub.
